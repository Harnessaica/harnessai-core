# HarnessAI — Brand Guidelines

## Tagline
"AI tools that work as hard as you do."

## Subheadline (website)
"Practical AI for trades and agricultural businesses in southern Alberta."

## Primary CTA
"Book a free 20-minute call."

## Tone of Voice
Plain spoken, confident, zero jargon.

**Write like a business owner talks.** If a grain farmer or plumbing contractor can't understand it, rewrite it.

- Short sentences. One idea at a time.
- No tech buzzwords: no "leverage," "synergy," "cutting-edge," "robust," "seamlessly"
- No AI hype: no "revolutionize," "transform your business overnight," "the future is here"
- Confident, not salesy
- Real problems, real results — specific over vague

**Good:** "Cut your invoice processing time from 2 hours to 10 minutes."
**Bad:** "Leverage cutting-edge AI to revolutionize your operational workflows."

## Visual Identity
- **Colors:** Deep charcoal or navy (primary) + warm amber or burnt orange (accent)
- **Typography:** Strong, clean sans-serif — readable at a glance
- **Photography:** Real Alberta — job sites, fields, real people at work. NO stock photos of people pointing at holograms or glowing screens.

## Website Structure
Four pages: Home, About, Services, Contact

**Home page:**
- Headline: "AI tools that work as hard as you do."
- Subheadline: "Practical AI for trades and agricultural businesses in southern Alberta."
- Single CTA: "Book a free 20-minute call."

## Key Message
Tyrell is not a tech consultant. He's a business owner who has done the hard work of figuring out which AI tools actually move the needle — and now helps other Alberta business owners do the same.
