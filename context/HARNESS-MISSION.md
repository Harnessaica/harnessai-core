# HarnessAI — Mission

## The Business
HarnessAI is an AI consulting firm based in Lethbridge, Alberta, Canada.
Domain: harnessai.ca
Founders: Tyrell Foreman, Jordan (jordan@youthone.ca)

## Who We Serve
Small businesses in southern Alberta — primarily:
- **Trades & contractors** — plumbers, electricians, HVAC, construction
- **Agriculture & rural businesses** — grain farmers, ranchers, agri-businesses

Go-to-market: direct outreach. Not inbound search. QR code on business cards drives to website.

## Why HarnessAI Exists
Most small business owners know AI exists but don't know which tools actually move the needle for their specific operation. They don't have time to experiment. They need someone who has already done the hard work of figuring out what works.

## The Differentiator
Both founders are active business owners who have implemented AI in their own operations. They're business owners talking to business owners — not tech people selling software. That credibility is the core of the brand.

Both come from running real organizations, making payroll, leading teams, and figuring out what actually works. Neither comes from a tech background. That's the point — they speak the same language as the business owners they serve.

## Definition of Success
A grain farmer or plumbing contractor walks away knowing exactly which 1-3 AI tools will save them real time — and either has them implemented or knows how to do it themselves.
