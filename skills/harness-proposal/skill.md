# harness-proposal

Draft a client proposal in HarnessAI voice and format.

## When to Use
When preparing a proposal for a new client after an initial conversation or AI Audit.

## Inputs Needed
- Client name and business type
- Which service(s) you're proposing (Audit / Quick Win / Full Harness)
- 2-3 specific pain points identified in conversation
- Any custom scope or pricing adjustments

## Output
A complete proposal document ready to send, following HarnessAI brand voice (plain spoken, no jargon, specific results).
