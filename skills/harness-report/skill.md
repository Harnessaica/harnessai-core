# harness-report

Generate a client-facing progress or results report.

## When to Use
At the end of a Quick Win or Full Harness engagement, or for 90-day follow-up check-ins.

## Inputs Needed
- Client name and project summary
- What was implemented
- Measurable results (time saved, cost reduced, errors eliminated)
- Next recommended steps

## Output
A clean, plain-language report the client can share internally or keep for their records.
