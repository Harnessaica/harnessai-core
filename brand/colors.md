# HarnessAI — Visual Identity

## Colors

| Role | Color | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| Primary dark | Deep charcoal | `#2C2C2C` (or navy `#1B2A3B`) |
| Accent | Warm amber | `#D97706` (or burnt orange `#C2410C`) |
| Background | Off-white | `#F9F7F4` |
| Text | Near-black | `#1A1A1A` |
| Muted text | Mid-grey | `#6B7280` |

_Note: Final hex values to be confirmed once full brand is designed._

## Typography
- **Headings:** Strong clean sans-serif (e.g. Inter, Montserrat, or similar)
- **Body:** Readable sans-serif at comfortable line height
- **No decorative or script fonts**

## Photography Style
- Real Alberta — job sites, fields, real people at work
- Natural light, honest settings
- **No** stock photos of people pointing at screens, holograms, or glowing AI visualizations
- **No** posed "tech company" imagery
